Accessible Cookie Notice enables your site to accessibly inform users that it uses cookies and comply with GDPR, CCPA and other data privacy laws.

== Description ==

Accessible Cookie Notices are a common requirement across the web. They are frequently intrusive and commonly not accessible to users with disabilities. Accessible Cookie Notice is a simple notice that takes accessibility into consideration at all points. 

Accessible Cookie Notice is not designed to be a richly featured or complex plug-in. It does not have a wide variety of bells or whistles. It is designed to provide an even and accessible experience for all users. 

= Accessible Cookie Notice Features: =

* Customizable notice message
* Multiple options for handling cookie expiration
* Link to Privacy Policy page
* WordPress Privacy Policy page synchronization
* WPML and Polylang compatible

== Installation ==

1. Install Accessible Cookie Notice either by uploading the files to your server.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to the Accessible Cookie Notice settings and set your options.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What makes this accessible? =

1. All controls use the appropriate type of control for their usage.
2. All controls have descriptive labels by default.
3. Color contrast is controlled by the plug-in to ensure meeting WCAG 2.0 requirements.
4. The visual position of the cookie banner matches its position in the source code.
5. The banner does not prevent access to other content on the screen.
6. Actions taken are announced for screen readers.
